2.5.4 Identify Distribution for Right Censoring(Pro)

ID-RC


Origin's Identify Distribution for Right Censoring uses maximum likelihood (MLE) to fit up to 7 common lifetime distributions.to your right-censored data, compares their fit using information criteria and probability plots, and helps you select the best-fitting distribution for subsequent parametric reliability analysis, survival estimation, and prediction of key quantities such as median life or failure probabilities at specific times.

  • Normal
  • Lognormal
  • Exponential
  • Smallest Extreme Value
  • Weibull
  • Logistic
  • Loglogistic
