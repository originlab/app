2.5.3 Warranty Analysis

SA-Warranty

The Warranty Analysis uses Survival Analysis to model and forecast field failures from censored post-sale claim data. By predicting future warranty costs and estimating spare-part demand, it can provide quantitative evidence for design improvement or service-policy adjustments.


Origin’s Warranty Analysis is integrated into the Reliability and Survival app.
