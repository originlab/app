2.5.8 Distribution Overview for Right Censoring (Pro)

RC-Dist-Overview

Distribution Overview for Right Censoring simultaneously fits up to 7 common lifetime distributions to your right-censored data using maximum likelihood (MLE), generates probability plots and goodness-of-fit statistics for each distribution, and provides a side-by-side comparison of estimated survival curves, hazard functions, and key quantities—such as median life, survival probabilities at any time, and the time when a given failure proportion is reached—enabling you to quickly evaluate distributional assumptions and select the most appropriate model for reliability analysis, contract specs, or future design change.

  • Normal
  • Lognormal
  • Exponential
  • Smallest Extreme Value
  • Weibull
  • Logistic
  • Loglogistic
