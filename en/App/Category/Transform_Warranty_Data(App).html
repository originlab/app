2.5.5 Transform Warranty Data

SA-Transform-Warranty


Transform Warranty Data converts raw warranty claim records—such as sales dates, failure dates, and production volumes—into a structured life table or interval-censored format suitable for reliability analysis, handles staggered entry and varying exposure times across production periods, and prepares your data for estimating survival probabilities, hazard rates, and key quantities such as cumulative failure proportions by age, enabling you to assess warranty performance, forecast future claims, and identify emerging quality issues for contract review or design improvement.
