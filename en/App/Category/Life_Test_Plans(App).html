2.5.11 Life Test Plans

RSA-LTP


Life Test Plans uses statistical precision and power criteria to determine the optimal sample size and test duration required to demonstrate reliability targets or detect meaningful differences in your right-censored life testing data, reports key quantities such as expected number of failures, test length, and precision bounds, and supports validation planning, contract specification compliance, and resource-efficient experimental design.

It include three parts

  • Demonstration
  • Estimation
  • Accelerated Life Testing
