2.5.10 Parametric Distribution Analysis (Arbitrary Censoring)

SA-Parametric-Dist-Arbitrary-Censoring

Origin’s Parametric Distribution Analysis for ArbitraryCensoring uses maximum likelihood (MLE) or least squares (LS) to fit up to 7 common lifetime distributions to your right-censored data, plots survival /hazard curves, and reports key quantities, such as median life, and survival probabilities at any time, or age when a given failure proportion is reached, for contract specs or future design change.
